The Alchemist is a small and independent art shop, born out of a passion for art supplies. We pride ourselves on our exceptional range of carefully selected artist materials.

We strive to support artists in their exploration, understanding and mastery of their medium and the development of their craft, by offering in-depth product information, technical advice, access to resources and some of the best artist materials from leading manufacturers and suppliers from Australia, Europe and the United States.

We also provide custom canvas stretching and archival picture framing for artworks, photographs, documents, objects and memorabilia.

Our picture framing offers contemporary style with museum quality at affordable prices. We believe not only in the presentation of an artwork, but equally in the preservation of the artwork.
